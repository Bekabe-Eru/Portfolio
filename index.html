About

// 02 — The Founder
JUDE SB

JUDE SB is the CEO and Founder of Amazed Studio, a creative practice built on one belief — that good design should make people stop, look twice, and feel something.

From brand identities to full-frame photography and digital experiences, every project passing through the studio is treated as a story worth telling properly. Amazed Studio works at the intersection of clean systems and expressive imagery, built for brands who refuse to blend in.

Based in Accra and working with clients worldwide, the studio's process stays hands-on from first sketch to final export.

JUDE SB Founder & Creative Director, Amazed Studio
